About This Project

This project is an exploration of colonial and gender bias in AI systems through the lens of theatre and performance.

  • Artistic Approach: Theatre and performance
  • Technology: Arduino-driven storytelling, interactive installations

The project aims to raise awareness about the impact of AI on society and promote inclusive practices in technology development.
